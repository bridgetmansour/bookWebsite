About Bridget Alexandra

Bridget Alexandra is on an exciting journey with her debut novel, "A Date with Death." What blossomed as a college idea has flourished into a passion project, where each word is meticulously crafted, embodying her dedication to quality. Her aim is to make her first publication not just special, but a reflection of her best self.

Away from the fictional world, Bridget works as a software engineer, where she brings stories to life through code. When she's not writing or coding, you'll find her lost in a book, catching some Z's, or daydreaming about the myriad hobbies she aspires to take up.

Bridget is a firm believer in lifelong learning, a journey she finds both enriches her professional skills and nourishes her creativity. Her path demonstrates that knowledge and creativity are deeply intertwined, each constantly fueling and inspiring the other.
